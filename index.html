Budget Planning

True cost of buying a home

Stamp duty is just the start. Here are all the costs first-time buyers often miss.

Stamp Duty
£0–£6,250+
First-time buyers pay 0% up to £300k. 5% on the portion from £300k–£500k.
Solicitor / Conveyancer
£1,500–£3,000
Includes searches, Land Registry fee, and legal fees. Always get at least three quotes.
Survey
£400–£1,500
RICS Level 2 (HomeBuyer) or Level 3 (Building Survey). Never skip this.
Mortgage Fees
£0–£2,000
Arrangement, booking, and valuation fees. Some lenders charge none — always compare.
Moving Costs
£300–£2,000
Removals company or van hire. Depends on how much you have and how far you're going.
Typical Total Budget
£3,000–£12,000
On top of your deposit. Budget for the higher end to avoid nasty surprises.
