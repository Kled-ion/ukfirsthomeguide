Budget planning

The true cost of buying

Stamp duty is just the start. Budget for all of these on top of your deposit.

🏛️
Stamp Duty (SDLT)
£0 – £10,000+
First-time buyers pay 0% up to £300k, 5% on the portion up to £500k. Must be paid within 14 days of completion.
⚖️
Solicitor / Conveyancer
£1,500 – £3,000
Includes legal searches, Land Registry fee, and professional fees. Always get at least three quotes.
🔍
Survey
£400 – £1,500
A RICS Level 2 (HomeBuyer) or Level 3 (Building Survey). Never skip a survey — it can save thousands.
🏦
Mortgage Fees
£0 – £2,000
Arrangement, booking, and valuation fees vary by lender. Some deals are fee-free — always compare the total cost.
🚚
Moving Costs
£300 – £2,000
Depends on how far you're moving and how much you have. Get at least three removal company quotes.
